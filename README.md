## What is WealthZen AI?

WealthZen AI helps you **track your money, plan your budget, and grow your savings** — all in one place. No signup. No app download. Just open and start.

The secret weapon? A built-in **AI Financial Advisor** that reads your actual spending and gives you real personalized advice — like having a financial expert in your pocket.

---

## ✨ Features

- 📊 **Dashboard** — See your income, expenses & savings at a glance
- 💳 **Transactions** — Log every expense with date & time
- 📅 **Budget Planner** — Set limits, get warned when overspending
- 🎯 **Savings Goals** — Track progress toward your financial dreams
- 🤖 **AI Advisor** — Get personalized money advice *(Premium)*
